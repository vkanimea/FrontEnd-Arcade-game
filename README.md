FrontEnd NanoDegree Arcade Frogger Game

Based on rubic (https://review.udacity.com/#!/projects/2696458597/rubric) for this assignment.

Instructions:
1.  Open the index.html to play game.
2.  Use the Arrow Keys to Move the player with the canvas.
3.  Avoid the Beetles travelling on the road and get across to the water as you have 3 lives.
4.  If you get hit by any of the beetles, you lose a life and this resets your position.
5.  As a Bonus watch out for a Star.
6.  To replay, press Ctrl + r
